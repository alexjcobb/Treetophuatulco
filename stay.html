The villa

Designed for calm, privacy, and connection

Four distinct suites, generous terraces, and open-air living spaces make TreeTop Villa equally suited to families, couples, and longer restorative stays.

The top-floor primary suite enjoys full privacy and direct access to the full-length deck and treetop island seating. All other bedrooms sit on the main floor with convenient access to terraces, the pool area, and shared living spaces.
